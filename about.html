About

I am currently a research fellow at the University of Southampton, working with Dr. Joao Mendonca on a next-generation planetary simulator called OASIS. In November 2025 I completed a research fellowship joining the Astronomy and Meteorology departments of the University of Stockholm, where I studied the climate feedbacks and habitability of rocky planets potentially observable with the conceptual Large Interferometer for Exoplanets. I am still an active member of the Bolin Centre for Climate Research.

Before coming to Stockholm, I completed my DPhil (Ph.D.) at the department of Atmospheric, Oceanic and Planetary Physics at the University of Oxford, where my work on atmospheric dynamics of pure steam post-runaway atmospheres was connected with the Oxford Network for Planets, and the EXOCONDENSE research programs. Before that, I completed my studies in Astronomy, Astrophysics, and Planetary Sciences at Sorbonne Université in Paris, France.
