About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Stockholm University. I study the evolution of the climates of rocky planets in order to improve our understanding of climate transitions and the loss or gain of habitability. My work is connected with the Oxford Network for Planets, and the EXOCONDENSE research programs.

Before coming to Stockholm, I completed my DPhil (PH.D.) at the department of Atmospheric, Oceanic and Planetary Physics at the University of Oxford. Before that, I completed my studies in Astronomy, Astrophysics, and Planetary Sciences at Sorbonne Université in Paris, France.
